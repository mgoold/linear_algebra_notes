$$
A\text{ = }
\left[ {\begin{array}{cc}
 1 & 5 & 7 & 9\\
 0 & 4 & 1 & 7\\
 2 & -2 & 11 & -3\\
\end{array} } \right]
$$

Answer:

$$
A\text{ = }
\left[ {\begin{array}{cc}
 1 & 5 & 7 & 9\\
 0 & 4 & 1 & 7\\
 2 & -2 & 11 & -3\\
\end{array} } \right]
\rightarrow
\left[ {\begin{array}{cc}
 1 & 5 & 7 & 9\\
 0 & 4 & 1 & 7\\
 2 & -2 & 11 & -3\\
\end{array} } \right]
$$

## Part 2. What is the rank of this matrix?

Answer: the rank of a matrix is its number of pivot variables = 2.
